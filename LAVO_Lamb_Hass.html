Lamb Hass

Description:

Size: The Lamb Hass sizes from Medium to Large

Weight: Can weigh between 200 to 300 grams

Shape: A distinctive Round shape

Skin: Unique Pebbly, Green to Purple-Black skin

Flavor: Beautiful Creamy Texture with a Rich, Nutty Flavor

Harvest Time: Typically harvested from Late Summer to Fall

Overview:

Lamb Hass delivers a creamy, nutty indulgence with a premium flavor profile, prized for its rich taste and strong market appeal.
